Italy






In Italy, The first case with COVID-19 was discovered, a 38-year-old man. The initial spot for COVID-19 was in northern Italy and outbreak of COVID-19 increased from northern Italy to the southern part and spread in all sides in Italy.

The most coronavirus cases are in Bergamo, a city in Lombardy.Piedmont is in the second place in coronavirus cases which means that there is extreme increasing in cities . The government imposed quarantined and closed all schools, universities, mosques , churches , cinemas , gym and amusement parks. And open supermarkets or restaurants (delivery only).

There are many activities that Italians do related to Italian culture but helped in COVID-19 spreading like outdoor markets, a lot of Italians buy their food from outdoor markets. Museums, where the Italian people are very interest about tourism. Many of people around the world came to Italy with a view to residence without knowing if they have the virus or not which led to outbreak of the virus among the population.

So please if you are an Italian citizen:

  • Stay at home as much as possible
  • if going out is necessary wear a medical mask
  • Avoid being in dense places in cities as they are known for widespread of the virus
